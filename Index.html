Alfonso Troyano Montes

My main strength lies in the design of robust and scalable software architectures, for which I have a wide variety of resources, both in design patterns and methodologies. Likewise, I am always in continuous learning to improve.
My main area of ​​interest is related to applications with relevant contributions to improve the quality of life and that, in turn, pose a technological challenge that makes me advance in my profession.
I started my professional career with a strong specialization in database management, implementing ETL (Extract Transfer and Load) capable of handling a large volume of data, through the use of very specific tools such as Trillium and DataStage. The experience gained, along with my training, allow me to design efficient databases, in accordance with the DataWarehouse philosophy, exploitables by both SQL and the external tools. The versatility of the implemented Jobs, have allowed to cover from complete database migrations to the daily or on request reporting.
In the railway sector, I have specialized in managing track models using the RailML standard for ERTMS level 1 and 2 signaling, I have worked on safety developments up to SIL-2. Current developments are being used in projects in operation, with a high level of availability and low error rate.
